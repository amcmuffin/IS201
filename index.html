Meet Adam,

Sup, I'm Adam McMullin! I am originally from Alpine, Utah, but after living for a few years in the great state of Alabama, I take great pride in my southern roots. I have recently returned from a two year church mission in North and South Dakota and am currently persuing a degree in marketing from Brigham Young Univeristy. Since a young age I knew my heart was in business and have worked hard to cultivate meaningful experiences to enchance my marketable skills. While in high school I worked as a content creator/photographer for several companies and on my church mission I was the head of social media for my area and wrote a handbook teaching others how best to utilize social media to improve church outreach. Currently, I work at the Sorensen Center for Moral and Ethical Leadership where I am head of the marketing team. Outside of work my hobbies include photography, sking, golfing, making out with my smokin hot girlfriend, and generally having a good time. Feel free to check my photography portfolio to see my style of photography fits your vision.
